If your Traditional IRA's value suffered a decline in 2022, this could be a good time to consider converting your Traditional IRA into a Roth IRA. By converting to a Roth IRA and paying the taxes on the conversion now out of other non-IRA assets, you can allow your IRA portfolio to recover in an account that is free from tax in the future. Thus, all of your market gains in your Roth IRA from the point of conversion forward will be income tax free. Please feel free to use this calculator to model different scenarios. Note that this calculator assumes zero state income tax, so if your state has an income tax, that will affect the calculation.
